What we do

Multiple ways to buy — so we can make a deal that fits your situation.

Most buyers only do one thing. We structure each purchase around what the seller actually needs, which means we can say yes where banks and traditional buyers say no.

01

We Buy Houses — As-Is

Sell without repairs, cleanouts, showings, or agent commissions. Inherited, tired rental, behind on payments, or just don't want the hassle — we'll make a fair offer and you pick the close date.

02

Creative Financing

Seller financing, subject-to, and lease-options. Spread your gain over time for tax efficiency, earn interest on your equity, and often net more than a discounted cash sale — without involving a bank.

03

Conventional Purchases

Prefer a clean, straightforward sale? We also buy with cash and conventional financing at fair market terms, with a fast, reliable close and proof of funds up front.

04

Income & Commercial Property

We also acquire and operate small income property — multifamily, RV parks, mobile-home parks, motels, and self-storage — across the region. Owner-operators welcome.
